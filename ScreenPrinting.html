Our year in New Media and Tech

New media and Tech is a required 9th grade course at the Engineering and Science University Magnet School where you learn and apply a diverse set of technology and design skills. In this class we work collaboratively to imagine new digital and physical art, animation, coding, and experiences. We code software and hardware to design and build real objects emerging into the world of virtual, augmented, and mixed realities.
